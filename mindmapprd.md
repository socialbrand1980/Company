# Product Requirement Document
Page: Growth Framework
Goal: Explain our structured system for growing brands through a clear visual framework.

---

## Objective

Create a dedicated page that visually explains our agency's brand growth system.  
The goal is to help potential clients quickly understand how we work and build trust by showing a structured methodology.

This page should position the agency as a strategic growth partner, not just a social media executor.

- Potential partners
