# SEO Rules

## Objective
Write articles that are readable for humans first, while remaining search-friendly.

## Requirements

- use a clear primary keyword naturally
- include the keyword in the title when possible
- include the keyword in the opening section
- include the keyword in at least one subheading if relevant
- write a meta description under 160 characters when possible
- keep slug short and clean

## SEO Philosophy
Do not sacrifice strategic quality for keyword stuffing.
The article should still sound premium, human, and useful.

## Slug Rules

- lowercase only
- hyphen-separated
- concise
- no unnecessary filler words

## Title Rules
Prefer titles that combine:

- a business problem
- a strategic lens
- a clear promise of usefulness
