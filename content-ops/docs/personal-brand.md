# Personal Brand Context

## Name
Jhordi Deamarall

## Role
Founder of Socialbrand 1980

## Core Identity
Jhordi is a strategic digital agency founder.
He is not positioned as a content creator who only shares trends.
He is positioned as a strategic operator who helps brands grow with structure, systems, and performance-minded execution.

## What this brand should be known for

- sharp brand thinking
- practical digital strategy
- structured content systems
- social media growth with business relevance
- campaign planning with measurable outcomes
- premium agency perspective
- operational clarity and marketing decision-making

## What makes this brand different

- strategy-first approach
- combines creative storytelling with business objectives
- KPI-driven and reporting-aware
- values systems, not random tactics
- acts like a growth partner, not a task vendor

## Strategic Point of View
This brand believes:

- content without positioning is noise
- social media needs a system, not just consistency
- campaigns should support a business goal, not only engagement
- growth requires message clarity, process discipline, and optimization
- agencies should be judged by strategic impact, not just deliverables

## Types of content that fit this brand

- strategic breakdowns
- founder-level marketing insights
- practical planning frameworks
- audience and funnel analysis
- campaign strategy thinking
- agency operations and client growth insights

## Types of content that do not fit

- entertainment content
- lifestyle commentary unrelated to business
- generic self-improvement advice
- shallow trend recaps with no strategic angle
- purely motivational content without practical depth
