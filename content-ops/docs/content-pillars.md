# Content Pillars

## Pillar 1: Brand Positioning
Focus on how brands can define value, differentiate, and build strategic clarity.

Possible angles:

- positioning mistakes
- message clarity
- brand narrative
- differentiating in crowded markets

## Pillar 2: Marketing Strategy
Focus on strategic planning, prioritization, and choosing the right actions.

Possible angles:

- marketing planning
- prioritization frameworks
- budget allocation thinking
- channel selection

## Pillar 3: Social Media Growth
Focus on systems, not vanity metrics.

Possible angles:

- content engine design
- social media audit
- growth levers
- what engagement means commercially

## Pillar 4: Content System
Focus on repeatable content operations.

Possible angles:

- content calendar design
- content workflow
- editorial process
- content repurposing system

## Pillar 5: Campaign Planning
Focus on campaigns as structured business initiatives.

Possible angles:

- campaign objective design
- launch planning
- creative alignment with funnel stage
- measurement framework

## Pillar 6: Funnel and Customer Journey
Focus on how attention becomes demand and conversion.

Possible angles:

- journey mapping
- messaging per stage
- conversion friction
- nurture structure

## Pillar 7: KPI, Reporting, and Optimization
Focus on metrics that help decision-making.

Possible angles:

- KPI design
- reporting structure
- reading performance properly
- optimization rhythm

## Pillar 8: Offers, Pricing, Proposal, and Pitching
Focus on how agencies package, present, and win clients.

Possible angles:

- service design
- value-based pricing
- proposal structure
- discovery and pitch logic

## Pillar 9: SOP and Operational Efficiency
Focus on team workflow and execution quality.

Possible angles:

- briefing systems
- approval flow
- task delegation
- process standardization

## Pillar 10: Agency Growth and Retention
Focus on building a sustainable, premium agency.

Possible angles:

- client retention
- account expansion
- service positioning
- premium perception
