# Visual Style Guide

## Purpose
Each article should have a visual direction that strengthens the SocialBrand1980 personal brand:

- strategic
- premium
- modern
- sharp
- business-focused

The image should feel like it belongs to a strategy-led digital agency founder, not a generic marketing blog.

## Core Visual Direction

- editorial and premium, not playful
- modern digital-business atmosphere
- clean composition with strong focal point
- confident and structured, not noisy
- cinematic but still useful for business content

## Brand Personality in Visual Form

- strategy-first, not trend-chasing
- smart and growth-oriented
- clear and decisive
- commercially mature
- premium but accessible

## Preferred Color Mood

- deep navy
- charcoal black
- electric blue
- cyan accents
- subtle white highlights

These colors should support a high-contrast, polished look.

## Preferred Cover Image Types

- abstract strategic visuals
- premium business editorial scenes
- modern workspace or planning environment
- dashboard, funnel, growth, or system-inspired compositions
- structured visual metaphors for positioning, workflow, planning, or optimization

## Recommended Composition

- clear main subject
- enough negative space for title overlay if needed
- strong lighting contrast
- clean layers and depth
- one dominant concept, not many unrelated objects

## What to Avoid

- generic smiling office teams
- cheesy corporate stock photos
- cluttered illustrations
- meme-like visuals
- random social media icons scattered without meaning
- exaggerated futuristic sci-fi effects
- childish or overly playful color palettes

## Topic-to-Visual Translation

### Brand Positioning
Use visuals that imply clarity, differentiation, identity, focus, or standing out in a crowded space.

### Social Media Growth
Use visuals that imply momentum, systemized growth, performance signals, content engine logic, or strategic visibility.

### Content Marketing
Use visuals that imply workflow, planning, editorial systems, briefing, documentation, or structured execution.

### KPI / Reporting / Optimization
Use visuals that imply dashboards, analysis, decision-making, signal reading, or performance review.

### Funnel / Customer Journey
Use visuals that imply stages, movement, conversion flow, guided progression, or bottlenecks being identified.

### Campaign Planning
Use visuals that imply orchestration, launch preparation, message alignment, or execution architecture.

## Image Prompt Pattern
When generating a cover concept, aim for:

1. article topic
2. business concept behind the topic
3. premium editorial visual style
4. SocialBrand1980 color mood
5. clean composition
6. realistic or semi-abstract business aesthetic

## Alt Text Rule
Alt text should describe the image clearly and specifically.
Do not stuff keywords.
Describe the subject and the business meaning of the image in natural Indonesian.
