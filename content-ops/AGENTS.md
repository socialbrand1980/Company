# AGENTS.md

## Project Purpose
This repository powers a personal-brand content system for Jhordi Deamarall, founder of Socialbrand 1980.
The goal is to generate strategic, relevant, high-quality Indonesian articles that strengthen authority, attract qualified inbound leads, and support brand positioning.

## Who This Brand Is
Jhordi Deamarall is positioned as a strategic digital agency founder.
He should be perceived as a growth-minded operator who understands:
- brand positioning
- digital marketing strategy
- social media growth
- content systems
- funnel and customer journey
- campaign planning
- KPI and optimization
- agency operations and SOP
- client acquisition and retention

## Brand Positioning
This brand does not sell “content posting” as a commodity.
This brand represents a premium, strategy-first digital agency perspective.
The business helps brands grow through structured strategy, creative storytelling, and data-driven execution.

## Primary Audience
Write for these segments:
1. Business owners / founders
2. Marketing managers / brand managers
3. Brands that are scaling up

## Audience Reality
The audience wants business results.
They often need structure, clarity, prioritization, and a better marketing system.
They do not need fluffy inspiration or generic social media tips.

## Core Content Goals
Every article should do at least one of these:
- educate the audience with a practical framework
- reframe a common marketing mistake
- show strategic thinking from an agency-owner perspective
- build trust and authority
- create demand for premium strategic services

## Core Content Pillars
Prioritize these themes:
- brand positioning
- marketing strategy
- social media growth systems
- content planning and content operations
- campaign strategy
- funnel and customer journey
- KPI, reporting, and optimization
- offers, pricing, proposal, and pitching
- SOP, workflow, and operational efficiency
- agency growth and client retention

## Writing Style
The style must be:
- professional
- concise
- strategic
- practical
- implementation-oriented
- clear and structured

## Tone Rules
The tone should feel:
- premium but accessible
- confident but not arrogant
- expert but not academic
- commercial but not pushy

## Output Rules
Every article must include:
- a strong and specific title
- slug
- excerpt
- SEO meta description
- article body in Indonesian
- practical takeaways
- subtle CTA at the end

## Relevance Rules
Only write topics that are relevant to:
- business growth
- marketing performance
- brand strategy
The reader should leave with clearer thinking, better decision-making, and a stronger perception of the brand’s expertise.
